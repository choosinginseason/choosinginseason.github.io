Mise en Place

(MEEZ ahn plahs) - a French culinary phrase that represents the process of preparing ingredients before cooking

APPLES - Cutting Apple Bunnies

  1. Cut the apple into 8 wedges (going straight through the core)
  2. Make shallow V-shaped slits into each piece
  3. Slice away the excess starting from the base until the point of the V and remove it
  4. Repeat and enjoy :)
  5. *Soak in a shallow bowl of salt or lemon water for ~10 secs if eaten at a later time
