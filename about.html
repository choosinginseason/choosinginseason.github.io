WHY IN SEASON?

  • Growing up as an Asian American, a lot of acts of affection are displayed with food. Fruit in particular hold a special place in my heart—from the way my ba ngoai (maternal grandmother) makes me dried persimmons from her trees, or the way my dad buys yellow dragon fruit even though they're expensive because he knows I like them
  • Coming to college I realized I had taken the fresh fruit & vegetables at home for granted. I never knew the ‘right time’ to buy certain produce and picking good produce was not a skill that I had
  • After moving into an apartment with my friends, I realized they were just as bad as me if not worse (one time I let my friend go grocery shopping by himself and he bought wilted green onions...)
  • Hopefully this site can help others in similar situations enjoy the taste of fresh produce :)

WHAT IS YOUR FAVORITE FRUIT / VEGETABLE?

  • Mangoes and steamed sweet potatoes or yams! They're perfect for cooling down in the summer or warming up in the winter

WHAT IS YOUR LEAST  FAVORITE FRUIT / VEGETABLE?

  • Ya' know I like to think that I'm not that picky, but I HIGHLY DISLIKE avocados. I honestly think avocados on toast is a waste of bread and would rather eat durian, but feel free to reach out and disagree with me on LinkedIn :)

HOW DID YOU GET ALL THIS INFO?

  • I actually picked up on a decent amount of how to pick out good produce by going grocery shopping with my parents as a kid, but I was still unfamiliar with a lot of things so I asked them when I went home for Thanksgiving haha
  • As for finding out what was actually in season I had no idea. In most supermarkets they'll still have most fruits and vegetables stocked even if they're out of season for the region (in this case CA). However, the trade-off is that they tend to be more expensive and it may be harder to find ripe produce readily available. So for this portion, I heavily refrenced Bay Area Seasonal Fruit & Vegetable Guide. It has a list of which produce are in season in Bay Area farmers markets which I thought would be nice for people who like to buy locally.

HOW DID YOU CHOOSE WHICH ITEMS TO INCLUDE?

  • Currently, this is not fully extensive site with ALL of the produce available in Bay Area markets. Due to the time constraints I decided to restrict it to seasonal items and exclude anything that could be bought year round.
  • Then to narrow it down I just choose what I liked to eat since I would be spending time to create the image library :')
