June

FRUITS

BLACKBERRIES

  • Ripe blackberries are plump, have dull skin, and a deep purple-blackish color
  • Make sure to check the bottom of the tray for mold!

CANTALOUPES

  • It might sound strange, but smell the melon! Ripe cantaloup will have a slight sweetish scent
  • Look for heavy dull melons with a golden colored rind (under the netting pattern)
  • Watch out for melons that have large soft spots or have cracks near the stem

VEGGIES

BROCCOLI

  • Should be deep green with compact heads and firm stalks
  • Avoid dried or browning stems and yellowing trees
